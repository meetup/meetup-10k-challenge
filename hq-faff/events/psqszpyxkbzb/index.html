Join us this Thursday at 6:30 for great food & drinks, insane views of the city, and plenty of IRL good times.

This event is every Thursday, weather permitting, until the end of summer.

Who should be invited?

- Orgs we love
- People we'd love to join our team
- People who should partner with Meetup
- Friends & acquaintances from other NYC tech companies
- Visiting family
etc...

Guests/Roof Rules (Good meetups always have good guidelines!)

- Guests need to check in at Meetup reception on the 10th Floor.
Be sure to include your guest(s) full name(s) when you RSVP so we can make it happen seamlessly. If your guest has not RSVP'd, keep your cell phone handy, because you'll have to meet them at reception.
- Show up for your guests: You're responsible for your guests while they're visiting Meetup. Guests need to be with you at all times, including on the roof and walking between floors.
-If you are interested in inviting more than 4 people, please alert Triforce so we can create a great experience for a larger group.
-We're doing this every Thursday, so if someone can't make it one week, invite them to the next one!

Reminder from last year: The building closes at 10 on Thursdays so we can reset and have a great Friday morning at HQ.
